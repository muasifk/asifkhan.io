About

Hi, I am Asif. I am a researcher with interests in wireless communication, distributed machine learning, edge computing and Internet of Things (IoT). I am passionate about Artificial Intelligence and the way it is transforming the world.

I studied in undergraduate at UET Peshawar and postgraduate at UET Taxila, both in Telecommunication Engineering. Lastly, I studied at Qatar University and received Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering. My Ph.D. supervisor is Prof. Ridha Hamila. I have worked as research assistant at Qatar Mobility Innovations Center (QMIC) and Qatar Transportation and Traffic Safety Center. I am interested in scientific review and welcome conference organizers and journal editors to send me review requests.

I am Member of IEEE Internet of Things (IoT) Community. I am volunteering as a community mentor at Coursera.
